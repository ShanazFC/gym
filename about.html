About Us

Who We Are:

Fun-N-Fitness is a recreational facility providing gymnastics and other sports' skills for students to gain both the physical skills as well as the confidence that comes with even the simplest of accomplishments. In order for our students to be successful, we build upon the basics to provide the best foundation for future skills. This will promote each student's learning and safety.

Our Philosophy:

Our philosophy is to teach gymnastics and other sports' related skills in a fun, safe and positive environment while at the same time raising each student's self-esteem.

Our Mission Statement:

Our mission is to provide movement education for students to gain awareness for physical fitness, which will promote positive lifestyle and fitness habits.
