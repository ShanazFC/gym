Birthday Parties

Our birthday parties provide the FUN! so you can relax and enjoy your child's birthday along with everyone.

Birthday parties are for children ages 4-10 years* and includes one (1) of the following choices:

Option 1

  • 60 Minutes of Age-Appropriate Gym Activities led by an Instructor.

  • 30 Minutes of Simple **Refreshments/Gift Opening if desired.

Option 2

  • 50 Minutes of Age-Appropriate Gym Activities led by an Instructor.

  • 40 Minutes of Simple **Refreshments/Gift Opening if desired.

For both options:

  • Birthday Child receives a FREE Fun-N-Fitness T-shirt as a gift from us.

  • Set-up and clean-up is included.

Please Note:

*We can accommodate younger ages and/or mixed ages providing parents participate with the children. This type of party is an Open Format with less directed activities. An instructor is present at all times.

**Refreshments Provided by parent/guardian. Please no red or grape juice/drink or anything that will stain.

Cost and Scheduling

Cost: $100 for up to 10 children -- (Birthday Child + 9 Friends).

$5 for each additional child up to a total of 16 Children.

  • $50 Deposit is required to secure party date and time.

  • The balance is due on the day of the party

  • Please note: We only accept cash as forms of payment for birthday parties*

Dates and Times:

  • Parties are scheduled on a first-come first-served basis according to our gym availability.

  • We will try to accommodate your schedule to the best of our ability.

  • Please call (574) 259-FUNN to check dates and times.
