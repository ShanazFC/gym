Directions

Mapquest Navigation/App. will help you locate us!!!

  • *Driving from the East on the US 20 Bypass: Take the SR 331 (Elm Rd.) exit and go North (right) about 1 mile to Dragoon. Turn Left onto Dragoon and go 1 mile to Fir Rd. Turn Right on Fir Rd. (Fir and Byrkit are the same road) and go 7/10th of mile. The gym is on the left. Gray building.
  • *Driving from the West on the US 20 Bypass: Take Bremen Hwy. exit, by the Meijer, and go North (left) about 1 mile to Dragoon. Turn right onto Dragoon and go 1 mile to Fir Rd. Turn Left on Fir Rd. (Fir & Byrkit are the same road) and go 7/10ths of a mile. The gym is on the left. Gray building.
  • *From Osceola: Go West on Lincolnway until Capital. Turn Left (South) on Capital. Turn Right on 12 St. Go 1 mile to Bykrit. Turn Left on Byrkit. Gym is the 3rd business on the right, passed the 7-Eleven. (Same side of 7-Eleven) Gray building.
  • *From Mishawaka: Go East on Lincolnway until Byrkit. Byrkit is 1st light passed Mishawaka High School. There is a Subway at this light. Turn Right onto Byrkit. Go over railroad tracks and next light is 12th St. Go thru light and gym is the 3rd business on the right, passed the 7-Eleven. (same side of street as 7-Eleven) Gray building.
  • *From Granger: Take SR331 (Capital Ave.) going South to 12th Street (1st light passed Lincolnway). Turn Right on 12th St. Go 1 mile to Byrkit. Turn Left on Byrkit. Gym is the 3rd business on the Right, passed the 7-Eleven. (Same side as 7-Eleven) Gray building.
  • *From North Mishawaka: Go South on Main St. to Jefferson. Turn Left on Jefferson & go to Cedar. (Jordan Ford Car dealership at this intersection) Turn Right on Cedar and take to Mishawaka Ave. (just passed Battel School) Turn Left on Mishawaka Ave. and follow to flashing light. Take right at curve as you are now on Byrkit. Go thru next light (Lincolnway). Go over railroad tracks and next light is 12th St. Go thru light and gym is the 3rd business on the right, passed the 7-Eleven. (Same side of street as 7-Eleven) Gray building.
  • *From South Bend: Go East on Ewing/Dragoon until Fir. (1 mile passed Bremen Hwy) Turn Left on Fir (Fir & Byrkit are the same road) and go 7/10ths of a mile. The gym is on the left. Gray Building.
