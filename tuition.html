Tuition

Tuition covers the entire session and is based on the number of classes per week each child/children per family enrolled.

  • Example: Your child takes 2 classes/week or you have 2 children taking 1 class each, tuition would be under the “2 Classes” price.

A $20 Annual Membership Fee is required for each student.

Students will not be allowed to participate in class if tuition is not current.

We only accept checks or cash as forms of payment. Unfortunately, we do not accept debit/credit cards. Additionally, post dated checks are not accepted.
